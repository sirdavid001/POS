Account deletion

Request deletion of your QuickPOS account.

QuickPOS account deletion requests are handled by SIRDAVID MULTI-TRADE LTD, the operator of QuickPOS.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your QuickPOS account and associated store data, email support@quickpos.com.ng from the owner email address on the account.

Include the following details so we can verify and process the request:

  • Store name
  • Owner name
  • Account email address
  • Phone number on the account, if available
  • A short statement confirming that you want the account deleted

What is deleted

After verification, we delete or anonymise account profile data, staff accounts, store settings, products, customers, inventory records, orders, reports, support messages, and other store records associated with the QuickPOS account, where deletion is technically and legally permitted.

What may be retained

Some records may be retained where required for legal, tax, accounting, fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, or regulatory reasons. This can include billing transaction references, payment provider records, legal acceptance records, security logs, and limited support history needed to prove that a deletion request was handled.

Retained records are kept only for as long as reasonably necessary for those purposes, then deleted or anonymised according to our retention practices.

Processing time

We aim to acknowledge deletion requests within 2 business days. Verified deletion requests are normally completed within 30 days, unless a longer period is required by law or needed to complete verification, resolve an active dispute, or meet regulatory obligations.

Questions

Privacy and deletion questions can be sent to support@quickpos.com.ng. See the QuickPOS Privacy Policy for more information about data handling and user rights.
