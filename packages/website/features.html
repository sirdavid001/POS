Store operations

A complete workflow from product setup to final report.

Each module is designed around the daily tasks that keep a retail counter moving: sell quickly, protect stock accuracy, keep records clean, and give owners clear visibility.

01

Point of sale

Run fast checkout with searchable products, barcode scanning, cart controls, customer selection, payment method tracking, and printable receipts.

  • Cash, card, and transfer payment method records
  • Camera, USB, and Bluetooth barcode scanning
  • Receipt printing with store header and footer support
  • Offline sale queueing when the connection drops
02

Products and inventory

Keep your catalog, prices, cost prices, categories, barcodes, images, and stock levels organized from one place.

  • Product, SKU, barcode, category, and image records
  • Stock quantities and low-stock thresholds
  • Inventory adjustments for stock in, stock out, and corrections
  • Supplier records and inventory change history
03

Orders and customers

Maintain a clean sales trail with customer details, cashier attribution, payment method records, order status filters, and CSV exports.

  • Walk-in and saved customer selection
  • Customer search, contact details, address, and loyalty points
  • Order history with cashier, customer, status, date, and totals
  • Detailed order views and downloadable order CSV files
04

Reports and owner statements

Give managers and owners a reliable picture of sales activity, revenue, products, and store performance.

  • Revenue totals for today, week, month, and all time
  • Revenue trend charts, recent orders, and top products
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly sales report filters
  • Store statements with print, PDF, Excel, and email delivery
