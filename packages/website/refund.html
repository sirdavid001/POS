Refund policy

Effective June 10, 2026. Review the pricing and product information before completing the initial activation or a renewal payment.

Cancellation

You may cancel an active recurring subscription from the QuickPOS billing screen. Cancellation stops future renewal and does not remove access already paid for; the store remains active through the current period end.

No prorated refunds

QuickPOS does not provide prorated refunds or credits for unused time in the initial five-month activation period, partial renewal periods, plan changes, cancelled subscriptions, or devices and staff not used during a paid period.

Duplicate or erroneous charges

If you believe you were charged more than once or charged in error, contact support promptly with the store name, payment provider, payment date, amount, and provider reference. Verified duplicate or erroneous charges will be reviewed individually and corrected where appropriate.

Provider timing

Approved refunds, where applicable, are returned through the original payment provider. Bank and provider processing times are outside QuickPOS control.
