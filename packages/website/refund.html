Refund policy

Effective June 11, 2026. Please review the pricing and product information carefully before completing the initial activation or any renewal payment. By completing a payment, you confirm that you have read and understood this policy.

Cancellation

You may cancel an active recurring subscription at any time from the QuickPOS billing screen without contacting support. Cancellation stops future automatic renewal and does not remove access already paid for; the store remains fully active through the end of the current paid period. After that period ends, the store enters read-only mode as described in the Terms of Service.

No prorated refunds

QuickPOS subscription fees are charged in advance for a defined period of access. QuickPOS does not provide prorated refunds or credits for:

  • Unused time in the initial five-month activation period;
  • Partial or unused portions of a renewal period after cancellation;
  • Plan changes, upgrades, or downgrades;
  • Devices, staff accounts, or features not used during a paid period;
  • Periods during which you did not use the service.

We encourage you to evaluate the service and review the pricing page fully before activating or renewing.

Duplicate or erroneous charges

If you believe you were charged more than once for the same period, or charged an amount different from the published price, please contact us promptly at support@quickpos.com.ng with the following details: store name and email address; payment provider (Paystack or Flutterwave); payment date; amount charged; and the provider payment reference. We will investigate and, where a duplicate or erroneous charge is confirmed, correct it through a refund or credit.

Technical failure before activation

If a payment is debited by the provider but your subscription is not activated within 24 hours due to a technical failure on our side, please contact support with your payment reference. We will either activate your subscription or arrange a refund for the unprocessed charge.

How to request a review

Email support@quickpos.com.ng from the store owner's registered email address and include the store name, payment provider, transaction reference, amount, payment date, and reason for the request. Do not send a card number, CVV, password, one-time password, or banking PIN. We may request additional information reasonably needed to verify the account and transaction.

Provider processing times

Approved refunds, where applicable, are returned through the original payment method via the payment provider (Paystack or Flutterwave). Reversal timelines are outside QuickPOS's control and typically take 5–10 business days depending on your bank.
