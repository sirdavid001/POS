Privacy policy

Effective June 10, 2026. This policy explains the information QuickPOS processes to provide store accounts, subscriptions, support, and software updates.

Information we process

We process owner and staff account details, store information, subscription status, billing references, app version and platform data, support messages, and operational logs. Hosted payment providers process payment authorization details; QuickPOS stores provider references and verification results rather than full card details.

Store business data

QuickPOS processes store products, orders, inventory records, settings, and reports to operate the service. Store data is isolated by store account. Store owners control staff access within their store.

How information is used

Information is used to authenticate users, deliver POS features, verify subscriptions, prevent fraud, send transactional messages, provide support, diagnose failures, and improve reliability.

Service providers

QuickPOS may use hosting, database, email, analytics, object storage, and payment providers, including Vercel, Cloudflare, Resend, Paystack, and Flutterwave. These providers process information under their own terms and security obligations.

Retention and security

Records are retained for as long as needed to operate the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and prevent abuse. We use access controls, signed sessions, provider webhook verification, and encrypted network transport, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

Store owners may update account and store information in the app and may contact support to request assistance with access, correction, or deletion, subject to legal and operational retention requirements.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to support@quickpos.name.ng.
