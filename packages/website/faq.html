FAQ

Straight answers before you start.

Still unsure about something? Email support@quickpos.name.ng and we will help.

Is QuickPOS free?

The download is free and every new store receives a seven-day all-feature trial. After the trial, choose monthly, quarterly, or yearly billing.

Does each staff member need a subscription?

No. One subscription covers unlimited staff and devices belonging to one store. Staff accounts inherit the store subscription and do not receive separate trials.

What happens when a subscription expires?

The app becomes read-only. You can still sign in, manage billing, view reports, print, export, and email store statements. New sales and other business-data changes are paused until renewal.

Can I use QuickPOS without internet?

The installed app may use its last verified entitlement for up to seven days offline, but never beyond the known trial or paid expiry date.

How do payments work?

Store owners choose Paystack or Flutterwave and complete payment on the provider's hosted page. Verified webhooks activate the store subscription.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Cancellation stops future automatic renewal. Your store keeps access until the current paid period ends. Plans are not prorated when changed or cancelled.

Can I install QuickPOS on iPhone?

The iPhone version will be distributed through TestFlight or the App Store when available. QuickPOS will not publish direct IPA downloads.

Why might Windows show a SmartScreen warning?

Early Windows builds may show a reputation warning until a code-signing certificate and download reputation are established. Always use the download and checksum published on this site.
